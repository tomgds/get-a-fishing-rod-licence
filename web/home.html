Fish with a rod: step by step

Find out what you need to do to be able to fish with a rod in England and Wales. There’s a different process in Scotland and Northern Ireland.

  1. Step 1

    1. Check when you need a licence
    2. When and where you can fish
    3. Check the fishing rules and bye-laws for your area
    4. Check the fishing rules for locks and weirs on the Thames

  2. Step 2

    You can get a 1-day, 8-day or 12-month licence for yourself or someone else.

    1. Buy a rod fishing licence £0 to £82

    You must get an extra licence to fish on locks and weirs on the Thames.

    1. Buy a lock and weir fishing licence for the Thames £20.50 to £31

  3. Step 3

    1. Find out about fish size and catch limits
    2. Read the rules around selling caught fish
    3. Report salmon and sea trout you've caught and returned
    4. Return a tagged fish
